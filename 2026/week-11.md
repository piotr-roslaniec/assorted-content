## AI & Software Engineering

- **[Something Big Is Happening](https://shumer.dev/something-big-is-happening)** — A well-balanced overview of AI's accelerating impact across industries and what it means for careers and daily life. Biased toward optimism but accessible enough for a non-technical audience — I shared it with a couple of friends and family members.

- **[Effective Harnesses for Long-Running Agents](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/effective-harnesses-for-long-running-agents)** — Anthropic's engineering team on building reliable harnesses for agents that run over extended periods — covering checkpoint strategies, error recovery, and resource management.

- **[How I Write Software with LLMs](https://www.stavros.io/posts/how-i-write-software-with-llms/)** — A practical walkthrough of one developer's coding setup and process with LLMs. Good introductory read for anyone building their own AI-assisted workflow.
